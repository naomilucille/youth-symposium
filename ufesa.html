Space Agency Objectives (UFE)


Water

  • The UF Space Agency aims to have 30% of water reserved for scientific research, after preliminary findings lasting as long as required.

Mining

  • The UF Space Agency wants preliminary findings to last as long as possible to identify natural and geological features of Mars in the space treaty.

Colonization

  • The UF Space Agency would like a hard cap of 100,000 people on Mars to limit human contamination in the space treaty.
