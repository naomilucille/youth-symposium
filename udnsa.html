Department of Defense (Military) Objectives (UDN)


Water

    *The UDN Space Agency wants water to be preserved for the study of Martian history. All water use should be approved by the UN, and no more than 20% of water reserved for colonization.

Mining

    *The UDN Space Agency wants mining rights assigned only to minerals needed for the continued exploration of space.

Colonization

    *The UDN Space Agency would like to see an international colonization project aimed at scientific discovery.
