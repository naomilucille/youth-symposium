Department of Defense (Military) Objectives (CR)


Water

  • The CR Military aims to allocate the largest amount of water possible to colonization for human consumption, and aims for 2% of water allocated to mining for human electricity consumption.

Mining

  • The CR Military aims to employ 0.5% of its population in military enforcement of mining, which totals to about 1 million people.

Colonization

  • The CR Military wants to allow military deployments of unlimited size, with limited weaponry. Wants colony population limited to 2% of the total size of each country on Earth for a period of 50 years.
