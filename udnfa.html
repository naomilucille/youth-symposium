Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) Objectives (UDN)


Water

  • UDN MFA wants 80% of water to be reserved for scientific research and UN approval for colonization and mining use.

Mining

  • The UDN MFA wants the majority of mining rights to be preserved until representatives from the UDN are present on Mars.

Colonization

  • The UDN MFA defers to the UDN Military in the matters of colonization, but looks unfavorably on any provisions which allow for CR or UF domination of Mars.
