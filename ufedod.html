Department of Defense (Military) Objectives (UFE)


Water

  • The UF Military defers to UF Trade Ministry and Foreign Affair Ministry; it is fine with overall mining demands for water as long as military forces can be self-sustained. However, it wants preliminary findings to last as long as required for safety purposes.

Mining

  • The UF Military seeks to establish a hard cap of up to 100,000 military personnel assigned to mining exploration and operation teams from The Ministry of Trade and Space Agency in the space treaty.

Colonization

  • The UFM wants to allow full military deployments to Martian colonies, with no restrictions outside of WMD.
