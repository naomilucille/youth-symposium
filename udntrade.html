Ministry of Trade Objectives (UDN)


Water

    *The UDN Ministry of Trade wants the creation of an international body to oversee all commercial development on Mars.

Mining

    *The UDN Ministry of Trade aims for a complete ban on the ownership of property on Mars. Leasing rights are negotiable.

Colonization

    *The UDN Ministry of Trade wants to establish that no tariffs should exist between Earth and Mars, or between any colonies. All Martian colonies should be treated as special economic zones and pay for mutual improvement of any all colonies.
