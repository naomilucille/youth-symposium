Anna Liu is a proud Texan. She graduated from Saint Louis University (’17) with her BA in Theatre, Political Science, and International Studies, with double minors in Chinese and Asian Studies. While at SLU, she was in the honors program, studied abroad in Beijing, and conducted research in China for her capstone project on China’s Two Child Policy. Anna will be attending graduate school this fall at Georgetown University’s School of Foreign Service for an MA in Security Studies. She is interested in US-China relations and diplomacy. When time permits, she volunteers with the American Legion Auxiliary’s Texas Girls State and enjoys cooking, traveling, and trying new things.
